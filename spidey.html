Marvel’s Spider-Man (2018)

Marvel’s Spider-Man (2018) is an open-world action-adventure game set in a vibrant, fictionalized New York City. Players control an experienced Peter Parker, balancing his life as Spider-Man and his personal challenges. The game features dynamic web-swinging, fluid combat, and a compelling storyline where Spider-Man faces off against the villain Mister Negative, who threatens the city with a deadly virus. Praised for its engaging narrative, stunning visuals, and immersive gameplay, it has become one of the best-selling PlayStation 4 titles.
